Manchester United, founded in 1878, is one of the world's most iconic football clubs, known as "The Red Devils." They play at Old Trafford, with a strong tradition of developing young talent. The club has won numerous domestic and international titles, making it one of the most successful in history.

Under Sir Alex Ferguson (1986-2013), Manchester United enjoyed its most successful period, winning 13 Premier League titles, 5 FA Cups, and 2 UEFA Champions League titles. Ferguson's era also saw the rise of the "Class of '92," a group of legendary homegrown players.
